Report: Land Cover Detection using Aerial Imaging


This project focuses on utilizing advanced machine learning techniques to analyse aerial imagery for accurate land cover detection. By leveraging high-resolution images captured from drones or satellites, the system can classify various types of land cover, such as forests, urban areas, water bodies, and agricultural fields. The goal is to provide valuable insights for environmental monitoring, urban planning, and resource management. This innovative approach combines computer vision and deep learning algorithms to process and interpret vast amounts of visual data, enabling efficient and precise land cover mapping.

Through comprehensive analysis and case studies, this work demonstrates how these cutting-edge technologies can revolutionise athlete development, optimise training efficiency, and provide coaches with actionable insights.
